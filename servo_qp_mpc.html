QP-MPC solves a constrained quadratic program online at each timestep: min ½UᵀHU + fᵀU subject to umin ≤ uk ≤ umax. When constraints are inactive, the solution matches the unconstrained LQR.
Progression: PID (SISO) → LQR/LQI (state-space, unconstrained) → QP-MPC (state-space + hard constraints). The QP formulation is the standard approach for handling actuator limits, current limits, and other physical constraints.
